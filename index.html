South Dakota MMJ Laws & Compliance

Age Requirements

  • 18-20 years: Edibles and non-inhalant products only
  • 21+ years: Full access to all cannabis products
  • Valid medical recommendation required

Possession Limits

  • 3 ounces of cannabis flower per month
  • Equivalent amounts for other product forms
  • Home cultivation: Up to 4 plants (if allowed)

Caregiver Rights

  • Can purchase for registered patients
  • Must maintain valid caregiver card
  • Same possession limits apply

Become a Patient/Caregiver

SD Residents:

  1. Obtain physician recommendation
  2. Complete state application online
  3. Pay $100 application fee
  4. Receive card within 30 days

Non-Residents:

  1. Must have valid medical card from home state
  2. Apply for temporary visitor permit
  3. Pay $50 temporary permit fee
  4. Valid for 30-day periods

Important Restrictions

  • No consumption in public places
  • No driving under the influence
  • No sale or distribution to others
  • Must keep products in original packaging
  • No consumption on federal property

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2024
Last Updated: November 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide medical cannabis tracking services:

  • Personal Health Information (PHI): Medical card numbers, physician information, qualifying conditions
  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, address, phone number, email address
  • Usage Data: Cannabis purchase history, allotment tracking, dispensary visits
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify medical cannabis patient status with South Dakota Department of Health
  • Track monthly allotment usage and compliance
  • Provide dispensary recommendations and price comparisons
  • Generate compliance reports for regulatory purposes
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • As required by law or regulatory authorities
  • To comply with HIPAA-covered entity requirements
  • With verified dispensary partners for transaction processing
  • For emergency medical situations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256 encryption for all PHI storage
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure
