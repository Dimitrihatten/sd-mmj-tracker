South Dakota MMJ Laws & Compliance

Age Requirements

  • 18-20 years: Edibles and non-inhalant products only
  • 21+ years: Full access to all cannabis products
  • Valid medical recommendation required

Possession Limits

  • 3 ounces of cannabis flower per month
  • Equivalent amounts for other product forms
  • Home cultivation: Up to 4 plants (if allowed)

Caregiver Rights

  • Can purchase for registered patients
  • Must maintain valid caregiver card
  • Same possession limits apply

Become a Patient/Caregiver

SD Residents:

  1. Obtain physician recommendation
  2. Complete state application online
  3. Pay $100 application fee
  4. Receive card within 30 days

Non-Residents:

  1. Must have valid medical card from home state
  2. Apply for temporary visitor permit
  3. Pay $50 temporary permit fee
  4. Valid for 30-day periods

HIPAA Compliance Statement

HIPAA Compliant Platform

SDMMJTracker is fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) of 1996.

1. Protected Health Information (PHI)

We handle the following types of PHI in accordance with HIPAA regulations:

  • Medical cannabis patient identification numbers
  • Qualifying medical conditions
  • Physician recommendations and medical records
  • Cannabis usage and dosage information
  • Treatment history and outcomes

2. Administrative Safeguards

  • Designated HIPAA Security Officer
  • Workforce training and access management
  • Regular security risk assessments
  • Incident response procedures
  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all vendors

3. Physical Safeguards

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Biometric and multi-factor access controls
  • Environmental monitoring and disaster recovery
  • Secure workstation and media controls

4. Technical Safeguards

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • Automated access controls and audit logs
  • Regular vulnerability scanning and penetration testing
  • Secure authentication and session management

5. Patient Rights Under HIPAA

As a patient, you have the right to:

  • Access and obtain copies of your PHI
  • Request amendments to your PHI
  • Request restrictions on use and disclosure
  • Request confidential communications
  • File complaints about privacy practices

6. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving PHI, we will notify affected individuals and regulatory authorities within 60 days as required by HIPAA regulations.
