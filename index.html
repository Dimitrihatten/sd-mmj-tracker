South Dakota MMJ Laws

Age Requirements

  • • 18-20 years: Edibles and non-inhalant products only
  • • 21+ years: Full access to all cannabis products
  • • Valid medical recommendation required

Possession Limits

  • • 3 ounces of cannabis flower per month
  • • Equivalent amounts for other product forms
  • • Home cultivation: Up to 4 plants (if allowed)

Caregiver Rights

  • • Can purchase for registered patients
  • • Must maintain valid caregiver card
  • • Same possession limits apply

Become a Patient/Caregiver

SD Residents

  1. Obtain physician recommendation
  2. Complete state application online
  3. Pay $100 application fee
  4. Receive card within 30 days

Non-Residents

  1. Must have valid medical card from home state
  2. Apply for temporary visitor permit
  3. Pay $50 temporary permit fee
  4. Valid for 30-day periods
