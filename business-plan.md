# SD MMJ Tracker - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

SD MMJ Tracker is a HIPAA-compliant technology platform designed to streamline medical cannabis patient verification and allotment tracking in South Dakota. Our solution addresses critical compliance challenges in the emerging medical cannabis market while ensuring patient privacy and regulatory adherence.

### Key Value Propositions
- Real-time patient verification system
- Automated compliance monitoring
- HIPAA-compliant data handling
- Seamless dispensary integration
- Healthcare provider collaboration tools

## Market Analysis

### Market Size
- South Dakota medical cannabis program launched in 2021
- Current registered patients: ~5,000 (projected growth to 15,000 by 2025)
- Licensed dispensaries: 15+ locations statewide
- Healthcare providers: 200+ registered to recommend medical cannabis

### Market Opportunity
- **B2B Market:** Dispensaries, healthcare providers, testing facilities
- **B2C Market:** Medical cannabis patients and caregivers
- **B2G Market:** State regulatory compliance and reporting

### Competitive Landscape
- Limited existing solutions in South Dakota market
- National competitors focused on larger markets
- Opportunity for local, regulation-specific solution

## Products and Services

### Core Platform Features
1. **Patient Verification System**
   - Real-time registry integration
   - Instant eligibility confirmation
   - Age verification and restrictions

2. **Allotment Tracking**
   - Monthly limit monitoring
   - Purchase history tracking
   - Compliance alerts

3. **Dispensary Integration**
   - POS system integration
   - Inventory management
   - Sales reporting

4. **Healthcare Provider Portal**
   - Patient management tools
   - Recommendation tracking
   - Compliance monitoring

### Service Offerings
- Platform licensing
- Integration services
- Compliance consulting
- Training and support
- Custom development

## Technology Infrastructure

### Security and Compliance
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- HIPAA compliance framework
- End-to-end encryption
- Multi-factor authentication
- Regular security audits

### Scalability
- Cloud-based architecture
- API-first design
- Microservices architecture
- Auto-scaling capabilities

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **SaaS Subscriptions**
   - Dispensary licenses: $500-2,000/month
   - Healthcare provider licenses: $200-500/month
   - Patient access: Free (ad-supported)

2. **Transaction Fees**
   - Processing fees: $0.10-0.50 per transaction
   - Verification fees: $0.25 per lookup

3. **Professional Services**
   - Implementation: $5,000-25,000
   - Training: $1,000-5,000
   - Custom development: $150-250/hour

4. **Compliance Services**
   - Regulatory consulting: $200-300/hour
   - Audit support: $5,000-15,000
   - Compliance monitoring: $1,000-3,000/month
