6. South Dakota Cannabis Law Compliance

6.1 State Regulatory Framework

Your use of this Service must comply with South Dakota Codified Laws Chapter 34-20G and all regulations promulgated by the South Dakota Department of Health regarding medical cannabis.

  • Maintain current registration with the South Dakota Medical Cannabis Program
  • Comply with all patient identification and verification requirements
  • Follow all possession and purchase limits as established by state law
  • Use cannabis only for the qualifying medical conditions specified in your physician recommendation

6.2 Possession Limits

Monthly Possession Limits:

  • 3 ounces of cannabis flower
  • Equivalent amounts for other product forms (concentrates, edibles, tinctures)
  • Home cultivation: Up to 4 plants (where legally permitted)

6.3 Age-Based Restrictions

  • Ages 18-20: Limited to non-inhalant products (edibles, tinctures, topicals, suppositories)
  • Ages 21+: Full access to all cannabis product categories, subject to physician recommendation
  • Valid medical recommendation from licensed physician required for all age groups

6.4 Caregiver Provisions

  • Designated caregivers may purchase cannabis on behalf of registered patients
  • Caregivers must maintain valid caregiver registration cards
  • Same possession limits apply to caregiver purchases
  • Caregivers must provide cannabis only to their designated patients

6.5 Reporting and Compliance

SDMMJTracker facilitates compliance reporting but users remain responsible for:

  • Maintaining accurate purchase records
  • Reporting any adverse effects to physicians
  • Cooperating with state inspections or audits
  • Notifying authorities of any violations or concerns
